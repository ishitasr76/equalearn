EquaLearn

"Education for All, Empowerment for Everyone"

EquaLearn is dedicated to fostering a positive social impact in our community by focusing on early access to education. It aims to aid underprivileged children worldwide and provide them with educational opportunities, specifically in the English subject. This program currently offers learning materials for kindergarten through second-grade students to facilitate practice and comprehension of vocabulary, parts of speech, and grammar. It lays a foundation of learning and empowers students, regardless of their backgrounds or circumstances, to thrive in the future.
